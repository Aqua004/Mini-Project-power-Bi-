About Project

Sales Data Analysis & Forecasting Dashboard is a Power BI project based on the Global Superstore dataset. It analyzes key business metrics like total sales, profit, and quantity, helping identify trends, top-performing categories, and loss-making segments.

The dashboard also includes time-based analysis and forecasting to predict future sales patterns.

Dataset:

Global Superstore (Retail Sales Data)

Tools Used:

Power BI, Power Query, GitHub, Vercel

Key Insights:

  • Technology category generates the highest sales
  • Sales show a consistent upward trend over time
  • Forecast indicates stable growth in upcoming periods
