About Project

Sales Data Analysis & Forecasting Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project built on the Global Superstore dataset. It analyzes sales, profit, and customer trends across categories, regions, and time, with a built-in forecasting model for future predictions.

Dataset: Global Superstore (Retail Sales Data)

Tools Used: Power BI, Power Query, GitHub, Vercel

Key Insights: Technology drives highest sales, some sub-categories incur losses, and overall sales show a steady upward trend with stable future forecasts.
